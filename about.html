Learn About Us

Household of Grace (HOG) Ministry is a Prophetic, Apostolic, and Revival movement founded by the guidance of the Holy Spirit for impartation, prayer sessions, in-depth teaching, evangelistic outreaches, healing, and restoration of hope to the hopeless. HOG is an interdenominational ministry, open to anyone who desires to develop a deeper relationship with Jesus Christ.

The mandate was given in the year 2014 to the visionary Pastor Ayodeji Akande, who currently serves as the lead pastor of the ministry, then called “The Elishas”. The team met weekly for prayers and teachings, a place of intimacy and collaboration with the HOLY SPIRIT. In the year 2016, the ministry was officially named “Household of Grace”.

In the year 2017, the visionary of the ministry had an open vision where he received the mandate for the ministry “equipping and empowering Ministers”. In the year 2018, HOG had the maiden edition of “The Encounter Conference/Retreat” a 12-hours meeting marked by intense prayer, teachings, and impartation where ministers and believers come together for spiritual sharpening. This conference has been held annually since then.

Household of Grace currently runs a weekly online program called The Upper Room Experience (started in the year 2019), a platform for teaching and praying where believers can grow spiritually.
